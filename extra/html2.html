Dr. T. Sasikala

Dr. T. Sasikala joined Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology as a Lecturer in the year 1997. She has completed her M.E. Computer Science and Engineering in the year 2001 in Sathyabama Engineering College affiliated to Madras University. She has completed her Ph.D. in the year 2009 in Sathyabama University. She has totally 29 years of experience in Teaching, Administration and Research. Her area of research interest is Computer Networks and Security.

Experience
  • Professor & Dean for School of Computing in Sathyabama Institute of Science & Technology from 10 October 2018 to till date.
  • Principal in JEPPIAAR SRR Engineering College, Chennai from June 2010 to October 2018.
  • Assistant Professor and Professor in Sathyabama University, Chennai from May 1997 to May 2010.
  • Lecturer in Noorul Islam Engineering College, Kanyakumari District from June 1993 to May 1997.
  • Programmer cum Instructor in BITS Information Technology, Nagercoil from May 1991 to May 1993.
Dr. S. Vigneshwari

Dr. S. Vigneshwari joined as a Lecturer in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology in June 2007. She has completed her PhD in the year 2016. Her research interests are Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Semantic web, Big data analytics, IoT, Data Science and Computer Vision.

Achievements
  • Worked for obtaining NBA accreditation by CSE department in 2020.
  • Prepared and submitted relevant CSE department documents for ARIIA, 12B, NIRF.
